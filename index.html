Stonebank's Start

Stonebank was born on December 17th, 1990. He began making music while he studied graphic design in college. He used the program FL Studio to start producing music. His music carrer really started in 2014.

Stonebank came on to the Monstercat scene in 2014 with the Song "Eagle Eyes" featuring Emel, pronounced ML. Yet that was not the first track Michael Stonebank released. His first tracks were under the name 'Modulate'. I did not know that, but it is great to know that he started producing music before I found his music.

All of his songs and all of his music makes me want to break into a dance routine, but I cannot for the life of me, dance worth a dime. Some of the songs are ones I looke forward to in my playlist. I always enjoy a go Stonebank song when I'm walking to and from my classes. Plus, if fills the silence for when I walk, it allows me ot just forget that I have other work in other classes. That is most likely not a good thing all the time, but it works some of the time.

Career

Stonebank's career lifted off in 2014 with the release of Eagle Eyes. It goes to show that over the past five years, Stonebank has made over thirty songs. All of these songs consist of a multitude of genres. Some of those being Trap, Drumstep, Drum and Bass, House, Hardstyle, Electronic, and Happy Hardcore.

As the genres above imply, the Song 'Ripped to Pieces',(Album cover pictured above), is considered Happy Hardcore, or Hard Dance. 'The Only One' is a nice song mixed with drums, vocals, and nice driving message of being alone on top of the world, in a good way. 'Drop Bombs' is what is classified as Drumstep, and it sounds exactly like it implies; drums with a dubstep beat. The bass drops are what define these tpyes of song, it just resonates with you and more often than not, you find yourself doing random movements with your hands.

These three rank in my favorites amongst Stonebank's songs. Anytime I see Stonebank on the Monstercat label, I know it is going to be a banger. Plus, these are songs that the bass drop is just as good as the rest of the song, build up and all. I predict Stonebank will ecxell above and beyond anyone's expectations. This man has a very strong path ready for him, and I look forward to more of his releases.

Fun Facts

Stonebank is one-half of the duo 'Zero Hour'. The other half is another Monstercat artist by the name of Darren Styles. They only released two songs called, 'Bass Drop' and 'Twilight'.

Stonebank has done six remixes of other Monstercat songs. Those being 'Hero' by Pegboard Nerds (2014), 'The Girl' by Hellburg (2015), 'Deeper Love' by Botnek & I See MONSTAS (2016), 'Cold Skin' by Seven Lions & Echos (2017), 'THE DROP' by Gammer (2018), and 'Dream in Color' by HALIENE (2018). Also, this song, Dream in Color, was the first time since Stonebank made any Dubstep song since 'Eagle Eyes' in 2014.

Stonebank collaberates with other artists bringing us some of his best work. On Apple Music, when you search Stonebank. his most popular song is called 'Stronger (feat. EMEL)'
